Automating Social Media with ZTRAMedia Tools

Posted on August 20, 2023

Automation plays a crucial role in digital marketing efficiency. At ZTRAMedia, I developed an Instagram Scheduler & Analytics Tool using Python and the Instagram Graph API.

This tool automated post scheduling, reducing manual workload by 40%, and provided in-depth analytics that improved engagement rates by 20%. The insights gained from the analytics allowed for better content strategies and audience targeting.

The tool was instrumental in growing Instagram pages, some of which accumulated over 12 million followers. This project highlighted the impact of automation in scaling digital marketing efforts and achieving significant growth.
