Leading a Team in Developing EcoTracker

Posted on September 15, 2023

As the team lead for EcoTracker, I guided a group of five developers in creating a mobile platform that helps users discover sustainable businesses.

We utilized Scrum methodology, holding regular stand-ups and sprint planning sessions to ensure effective collaboration and progress tracking. My role involved coordinating tasks, facilitating communication, and resolving any obstacles the team faced.

We designed a personalized recommendation algorithm that increased user engagement by 30%. Integrating the OpenStreetMap API allowed us to provide eco-conscious routes and destinations, enhancing the user experience.

This project taught me valuable lessons in leadership, teamwork, and project management, which I look forward to applying in future endeavors.
